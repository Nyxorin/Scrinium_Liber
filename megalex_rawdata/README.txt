MEGALEX raw data
================

_Megalex_ (Ferrand et al. 2017) provides visual and auditory lexical decision times and accuracy rates several thousands of words: Visual lexical decision data are available for 28466 French words and the same number of pseudowords, and auditory lexical decision data are available for 17876 French words and the same number of pseudowords.

The two files 

    megalex_rawdata_auditory.csv
    megalex_rawdata_visual.csv

contain the raw data (trial level) from the *Megalex study*.

Processed data (cleaned and averaged by items) are also available :

* [Megalex-items-visual.tsv](http://www.lexique.org/databases/Megalex/Megalex-items-visual.tsv)
* [Megalex-items-auditory.tsv](http://www.lexique.org/databases/Megalex/Megalex-items-auditory.tsv)

[Online access](http://www.lexique.org/shiny/openlexicon) | [Openlexicon](http://chrplr.github.io/openlexicon)


If you use these data, you are required to cite:

Ferrand, Ludovic, Alain Méot, Elsa Spinelli, Boris New, Christophe Pallier, Patrick Bonin, Stéphane Dufau, Sebastiaan Mathôt, and Jonathan Grainger. 2017. “MEGALEX: A Megastudy of Visual and Auditory Word Recognition.” _Behavior Research Methods_. https://doi.org/10.3758/s13428-017-0943-1. ([pdf](Ferrand et al. - 2017 - MEGALEX A megastudy of visual and auditory word r.pdf))
